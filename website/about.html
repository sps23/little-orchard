Little Orchard was founded with a simple dream: to create a preschool where children feel safe, happy, and inspired to learn every day. Nestled in the heart of Naas, Co. Kildare, we've been helping young minds blossom since our doors first opened. Our warm, family-focused approach brings together the best of modern early-years education with timeless values of kindness, curiosity, and respect.

Our Core Values

💚

Kindness First

We foster empathy and respect in every interaction, helping children develop strong social skills and emotional intelligence from their earliest years.

🌱

Growth Through Play

Play is the foundation of our curriculum. Through guided and free play, children discover the joy of learning naturally and build resilience.

🌍

Inclusivity

Every child is unique and celebrated. We embrace diversity and create an environment where all backgrounds and abilities are welcomed and supported.

🤝

Partnership with Parents

We believe in working hand-in-hand with families. Regular updates, open-door policy, and parent events keep you connected to your child's journey.

🔬

Curiosity & Discovery

Our learning spaces are designed to spark wonder — from our science corner to the orchard garden, every area invites exploration.

🛡️

Safety & Wellbeing

Fully Tusla-registered with comprehensive safeguarding policies, first-aid trained staff, and secure, child-friendly premises.
