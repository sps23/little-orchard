Why Choose Little Orchard?

We combine the best of early-years education with a warm, family-like atmosphere that helps children thrive.

🎨

Creative Learning

Our play-based curriculum sparks imagination through art, music, storytelling, and hands-on exploration.

🌿

Outdoor Adventures

Children enjoy our beautiful orchard garden with sensory trails, mud kitchens, and nature discovery areas.

👩‍🏫

Qualified Educators

Our passionate team holds Level 7 and Level 8 qualifications in Early Childhood Education and Care.

🏡

Home-from-Home Feel

Small group sizes ensure every child receives individual attention in a cosy, welcoming environment.

🍎

Healthy Meals

Freshly prepared, nutritious meals and snacks made on-site daily with locally sourced ingredients.

📋

ECCE Approved

Fully registered with Tusla and ECCE scheme, offering two free preschool years for eligible children.
