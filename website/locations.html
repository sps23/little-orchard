Getting Here

We're located on Main Street in the centre of Naas, just a 5-minute walk from the town square. Free parking is available on-site for drop-off and collection. The M7 motorway (Junction 10) is only 3 minutes away, making us easily accessible from Newbridge, Sallins, Kill, and Clane.
