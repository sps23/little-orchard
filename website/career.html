At Little Orchard, we believe that great childcare starts with great people. If you're passionate about early childhood education, love working with children, and want to be part of a supportive and inspiring team, we'd love to hear from you.

Why Work With Us?

  • Competitive salary with annual reviews and progression opportunities
  • Supportive, friendly team culture — we genuinely look after each other
  • Continuous professional development and funded training opportunities
  • Access to the latest resources and a beautifully equipped learning environment
  • Generous annual leave and paid sick leave
  • Free meals during working hours — freshly prepared on-site every day
  • Employee Assistance Programme (EAP) for wellbeing support
  • The joy of making a real difference in children's lives every single day
