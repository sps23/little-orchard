At Little Orchard, we make enrolling your child as simple and stress-free as possible. Whether you're looking for our ECCE-funded sessional programme or full-day childcare, we'd love to welcome your little one into our community.

Why Enrol at Little Orchard?

  • Two free ECCE preschool years for children aged 2 years 8 months to 5 years 6 months
  • NCS (National Childcare Scheme) subsidies accepted — making quality childcare affordable
  • Play-based Aistear and Síolta curriculum delivered by qualified educators
  • Small class sizes (maximum 11 children per room) for individual attention
  • Freshly cooked, nutritious meals and snacks included at no extra cost
  • Beautiful outdoor learning spaces with a dedicated orchard garden
  • Flexible scheduling options — sessional, part-time, and full-day care
  • Regular progress updates through our parent communication app
  • Fully Tusla-registered with excellent inspection reports
  • Warm, inclusive, and culturally diverse learning environment
